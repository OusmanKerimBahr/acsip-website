About ACSIP

Association of Chadian Students in Pune

The Association of Chadian Students in Pune (ACSIP) is a community that connects and supports Chadian students studying in Pune. Our goal is to promote unity, cultural exchange and academic success among our members.

We organise social events, cultural celebrations and academic support activities to help students feel at home while studying abroad.
